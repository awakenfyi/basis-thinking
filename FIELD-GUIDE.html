Basis* Thinking
Tell · See · Prove · Do — the grammar of moving a stranger to action · field guide v1

Every communication that must move a stranger from ignorance to action carries four burdens. Basis reads any asset against them — and builds any asset from them. Two decades of hand practice, now specified for people and agents.

Role is one of three simultaneous coordinates:

Narrative role
Tell / See / Prove / Do — what is this module doing in the story?
Reader's question
What is it / Why care / How does it work / Who else — what is the visitor silently asking?
Experience intent
Guide / Educate / Assure / Inspire — what should they feel here?

Each layer catches failures the others miss: layer 1 finds the missing Prove; layer 2 finds the Prove that answers nobody's question; layer 3 finds nine Educates in a row with no Assure at the moment of friction.

The diagonal is the default; off-diagonal is stance. Deviations are where brands differentiate — and every deviation is declared with a one-line reason, never landed by accident.

The expansion map — bigger than marketing

The pitch

Vision / product / traction / terms. Most failed raises: Tell×Inspire stacked, Prove too late.

Hiring — both directions

Job posts and resumes carry the same four burdens. Most resumes are all Tell.

Product surfaces

Onboarding, empty states, pricing, changelogs — an empty state is a micro-page.

Education

Hook / demonstrate / evidence / exercise. Great teachers work the off-diagonal.

Science comms

Physical space

Packaging, booths, exhibits, menus. The grammar predates the web and outlives it.

Film & media

The trailer: story, footage, laurels, date.

Politics & civic

The stump speech and the ballot measure — burdens unchanged, stakes raised.

AI agent outputs — the frontier

An agent's report is a Basis asset: finding (Tell) · artifact (See) · counts, logs, hashes (Prove) · next action (Do). An agent that Tells without Proving is the oldest failure wearing new clothes. Every AI that reports to a human needs this grammar; almost none have it.

The jurisdiction test: does a stranger arrive ignorant and need to leave acting? If yes, the four burdens exist and Basis reads it. If no — a diary, a poem — Basis has no jurisdiction, and saying so is part of the method.
