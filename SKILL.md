# Basis Thinking — the runnable skill

Read any asset (page, deck, post, README, agent report) as a sequence of four module roles and
produce a lo-fi read-out: how it tells its story, how the field does it, where the gaps are.

## Procedure

1. **Fetch live, cite dated.** Never audit from memory. Verbatim quotes with URL + date-seen;
   paraphrase is where audit-slop begins. Unreachable channel → NOT SAMPLED, never filled in.
2. **Segment and tag.** Walk the asset top to bottom. Each module gets three coordinates:
   role (Tell/See/Prove/Do) × reader-question (What/Why/How/Who-else) × intent
   (Guide/Educate/Assure/Inspire). Tag off-diagonal pairs loudly — they are stance or error.
3. **Count, don't vibe.** Modules per role · seconds-to-first-See · claims vs receipts
   (the Tell-vs-Prove overhang) · CTA tiers present (T1 Build / T2 Buy / T3 Prove-advance / T4 Browse).
4. **The gap.** Where does Tell outrun Prove? Which burden is missing, and is the omission
   declared anywhere? One paragraph, evidence-labeled (anchor / consensus / story).
5. **The read-out.** Lo-fi, hierarchy from value (templates/readout.html). Field studies add
   the sea-of-sameness: what every competitor says identically, and the unclaimed seat —
   labeled story until tested.
6. **Steal or Invent** (the judgment). For each benchmark pattern found: adopt it or hold a
   distinct position — one line of reasoning either way, logged. Never adopt silently; never
   diverge accidentally.

## Conduct

- Taste verdicts stay labeled as taste; counts stay counts. A tool that calls a composition
  "bad" with false objectivity has failed regardless of accuracy.
- Every Tell claim in anything this skill *produces* carries an asterisk resolving to its
  Prove receipt in the same asset.
- The skill's own outputs are Basis assets: finding (Tell) · artifact (See) · counts (Prove) ·
  next step (Do). Grade them the same way.
